1. COMPANY OVERVIEW

About Contoso Electronics
Contoso Electronics was founded in 1995 and has grown to become a leading provider of consumer electronics and innovative technology solutions. Our mission is to enhance people's lives through cutting-edge technology and exceptional customer service.

Our headquarters is located in Seattle, Washington, with additional offices in Austin, Texas, and Atlanta, Georgia. We employ over 5,000 people worldwide and serve customers in more than 50 countries.

Company Values
- Innovation: We constantly push the boundaries of what's possible
- Quality: We deliver products and services that exceed expectations
- Integrity: We conduct business with honesty and transparency
- Customer Focus: Our customers are at the center of everything we do
- Teamwork: We achieve more when we work together

2. EMPLOYMENT POLICIES

Equal Opportunity Employment
Contoso Electronics is an equal opportunity employer. We do not discriminate based on race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, or any other protected characteristic.

Working Hours
Standard business hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM Pacific Time. Some departments may have flexible schedules or remote work options available with manager approval.

Dress Code
Business casual attire is expected in all office locations. On Fridays, employees may dress casually. Remote workers should maintain professional appearance during video calls.

3. BENEFITS AND COMPENSATION

Health Insurance
Contoso Electronics provides comprehensive health insurance coverage including medical, dental, and vision insurance. The company covers 80% of the premium costs for employees and 60% for dependents.

Retirement Plan
All full-time employees are eligible for our 401(k) retirement plan after 90 days of employment. The company matches up to 6% of employee contributions.

Paid Time Off
- Vacation: 15 days per year for the first 5 years, 20 days after 5 years
- Sick Leave: 10 days per year
- Personal Days: 3 days per year
- Holidays: 12 company holidays per year

Professional Development
Each employee receives $2,000 annually for professional development activities including conferences, training courses, and certifications.

4. WORK SCHEDULE AND TIME OFF

Flexible Work Arrangements
Contoso Electronics supports work-life balance through flexible arrangements:
- Remote work options available for eligible positions
- Flexible start times between 7:00 AM and 10:00 AM
- Compressed work weeks (4x10 schedule) available with manager approval

Time Off Requests
All time off requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least two weeks in advance. Emergency time off should be reported to your direct supervisor as soon as possible.

5. CODE OF CONDUCT

Professional Behavior
All employees are expected to maintain the highest standards of professional conduct:
- Treat all colleagues, customers, and partners with respect
- Maintain confidentiality of company information
- Avoid conflicts of interest
- Report any unethical behavior through proper channels

Anti-Harassment Policy
Contoso Electronics has zero tolerance for harassment of any kind. Any incidents should be reported immediately to HR or through our anonymous hotline: 1-800-CONTOSO.

6. IT AND SECURITY POLICIES

Computer and Network Usage
- Company computers and networks are for business use only
- Personal use is limited and must not interfere with work responsibilities
- All software installations must be approved by IT department
- Passwords must be changed every 90 days and meet complexity requirements

Data Protection
- All employees must complete annual security training
- Confidential data must be stored on approved company systems only
- USB drives and external storage devices require IT approval
- Report any suspected security incidents immediately to IT Security

7. SAFETY GUIDELINES

Office Safety
- Keep workspaces clean and organized
- Report any safety hazards immediately
- Know the location of fire exits and emergency procedures
- Participate in all required safety training

Ergonomics
- Adjust your workstation to prevent strain and injury
- Take regular breaks from computer work
- Request ergonomic assessments if experiencing discomfort
- Use proper lifting techniques for heavy items

8. PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT

Training Opportunities
Contoso Electronics is committed to employee growth and development:
- New employee orientation program
- Technical skills training
- Leadership development programs
- Cross-functional project opportunities
- Mentorship programs

Performance Reviews
- Annual performance reviews conducted in January
- Mid-year check-ins in July
- Goal setting and career planning discussions
- 360-degree feedback process for leadership roles

Tuition Reimbursement
Full-time employees may receive up to $5,000 per year for job-related education expenses including:
- Degree programs
- Professional certifications
- Industry conferences
- Online learning platforms
